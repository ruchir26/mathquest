CardQuest

Trading Card Battles · Powered by Math

How to Play

  1. 🎯 Goal: Reduce your opponent's HP from 20 to 0.
  2. 🎴 Each turn: Draw 1 card, gain +1 mana (max 10).
  3. ⚔️ Creatures stay on the board and attack each turn.
  4. ✨ Spells have a one-time effect.
  5. 🧮 Math Cards let you solve a puzzle to power up the effect — right answer = full power, wrong = half.
  6. 🌪️ Element wheel: Fire → Air → Earth → Water → Spirit → Fire. Beat the next element for +1 damage!
  7. 📦 Packs: Win battles to earn packs. Solve 3 puzzles to open them — the better you do, the rarer the cards.
